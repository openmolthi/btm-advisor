# SP4: Manage and Govern AI Agents with SAP LeanIX & SAP Signavio

## Official Reference: SAP Help Portal
- LeanIX EA Management: https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/
- Signavio Process Insights: https://help.sap.com/docs/signavio-process-insights/user-guide/introduction

## Meaning

Manage the end-to-end AI agent lifecycle for maximum impact: discover where agents bring value and where they are in use, give them the right context and guardrails, govern them to ensure compliance and control, and optimize value and performance over time.

## Core Pillars

- **The right agents:** where needed and grounded in the right business context
- **Working together:** with your people (not just technology)
- **Solving complex business challenges** that span business and processes

## Elevator Pitch

### 30 Seconds
Agentic AI is accelerating, and questions abound: where should agents be used, which agents are actually in play, and how do we keep them reliable, compliant, and valuable over time? With SAP LeanIX and SAP Signavio, you can manage the end-to-end AI agent lifecycle — discover, deploy with context, govern for compliance, and optimize performance and value.

### 90 Seconds
Start by **discovering** all agents — SAP, third-party, and custom — and identifying where they bring the highest value. Then **conceptualize and deploy** by mapping agents to enterprise architecture and grounding them with process models, business logic, and guardrails. **Govern** by monitoring for drift, misuse, or policy violations and triggering corrections. Finally, **optimize** by tracking adoption, scaling, redundancy, and quantifying business impact with executive dashboards.

## AI Agent Hub — Governance Layer

The AI Agent Hub is the central operational layer for agent inventory and controls. It bridges discovery with governance through automated registration and policy enforcement.

**Key Mechanics:**
- Agent card discovery (A2A protocol)
- Dedicated API & Registry
- Agent Fact Sheets (Type / Risk / Approval)

**Executive KPIs:**
- Number of approved agents
- Agent adoption rates
- Estimated time saved

## Lifecycle Model
