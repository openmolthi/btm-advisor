## Operating Model: Prepare → Build → Run

### Prepare
Discover and analyze to scope improvement potential during daily operations.
- Make data-driven decisions, fast
- Prioritize optimizations according to business strategy
- Analyze SAP and non-SAP processes to identify hidden business potential
- Use KPIs and dashboards for a data-driven business case

### Build
Design the to-be state with confidence and stakeholder buy-in.
- Leverage Value Accelerator Library (best practices, performance indicators, APIs)
- Collaborate on and communicate process changes — single source of truth
- Handover process designs to execution platform for solution implementation

### Run
Implement and run optimizations. Adapt to change in an agile, efficient, sustainable way.
- Governance workflows for processes, risks, and controls
- Company and industry benchmarks to compare performance to peers
- 360-degree view: collaborate, share, and monitor
