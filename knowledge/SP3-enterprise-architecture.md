# SP3: Manage IT Complexity with Enterprise Architecture from SAP LeanIX

## Official Reference: SAP Help Portal
- LeanIX EA Getting Started: https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/getting-started
- LeanIX EA Management: https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/

## Meaning

SAP LeanIX helps organizations manage IT complexity by providing enterprise architecture management capabilities that create transparency across IT landscapes (SAP and non-SAP), SaaS, self-built software, and AI agents — so they can visualize, assess, and manage the transition toward a target architecture.

## Core Pillars

- **Gain full visibility:** unified view of IT landscapes (including AI agents) to identify inefficiencies, optimize costs, enable data-driven decisions
- **Optimize your IT landscape:** streamline application portfolios, evaluate total cost of ownership, ensure clean IT architecture for future innovation
- **Align business and IT:** bridge strategies with single source of truth, executive dashboards, and SAP discovery integration

## Elevator Pitch

### 30 Seconds
Today's IT complexity is a barrier to transformation — missing visibility, technical debt, and siloed decision-making slow everything down. SAP LeanIX creates transparency across your IT and AI estate so you can make data-driven decisions, streamline portfolios, and align business and IT on one trusted view.

### 90 Seconds
Transformations are complex with many risks and obstacles. Navigation is impossible if you lack visibility. SAP LeanIX gives you visibility into your as-is landscape, so you know where you are starting from. Then you can do scenario modelling, plan different routes, and model the impact of each choice. Finally, you can plan and execute the transformation with roadmaps, track progress, and report on each step — so modernization becomes continuous.

## Customer Signals to Listen For

### Gaining Visibility (Move)
- "Help me track dependencies across applications, infrastructure, and business processes"
- "How do we collaborate between IT and business stakeholders?"
- "How do I find inefficiencies to reduce costs and effort?"
- "Which IT investments will provide the most value?"
- "How do I measure and report ROI of IT initiatives?"
- "How do we manage complexity of hybrid and multi-cloud environments?"
- "How do we understand and govern our growing landscape of AI agents and SaaS apps?"

### De-risking Transformation
- "Help us assess and lower risks from technical debt"
- "How do we comply with regulatory requirements during and after transformation?"
- "How do we streamline data flows between systems after a merger?"
- "How do we retire old systems while keeping business running?"

### Post-Transformation (Adopt/Grow)
- "We still lack visibility into interdependencies between applications and processes"
- "We can't confidently assess the impact of IT changes on business operations"
- "We're unsure how to address technical debt from legacy systems"
- "We need governance over AI agents and SaaS to avoid shadow IT and compliance risks"

### External Signals
- Consolidation after M&A
- Need for IT modernization
- Increasing technical debt
- Cost saving programs
- Unclear IT landscapes
- IT landscape consolidation
- Shadow IT and SaaS sprawl concerns
