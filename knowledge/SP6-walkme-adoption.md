# SP6: WalkMe for Enterprise-wide Digital Adoption (Secondary)

> **Note:** WalkMe is secondary for the SSE team. Rarely engaged but possible.

## Meaning

Help customers navigate constant technology change and ensure people adopt critical business processes and features across SAP Business Suite and non-SAP applications.

## Core Pillars

- Organizations adopt new tech faster than employees can use it effectively → the "digital adoption gap"
- WalkMe pinpoints and resolves digital friction inside workflows so companies achieve ROI
- **What WalkMe does:** guides users in the flow of work (in-app), identifies where people struggle, removes friction, prevents software from slowing revenue, burdening IT, or creating compliance risk

## Discovery Questions (SPIN Framework)

**Situation:** What key platforms do you use and how many users?

**Problem:** How many processes are users tasked with following across all systems? Any areas where adoption has been slow or ROI not achieved?

**Implication:** Take one critical business process — if users can't perform it consistently, accurately, and on time, what is the impact? Are you able to update users efficiently in line with cloud releases?

**Need:** If we help people complete that process accurately every time — what would you save and gain? What would be the impact of a successful digital adoption focus?
