What is process mining and why should a Japanese manufacturer care?

Process mining is like a digital X-ray for business operations. It analyzes system logs to show how processes actually run — not how people think they run.

🏭 Why it matters for Japanese manufacturers:

  • Labor shortage — find inefficiencies fast instead of hiring more people
  • 2025 cliff — S/4HANA migration requires understanding current processes first
  • Monozukuri culture — process mining is digital kaizen at scale

💡 Sales tip: Lead with "digital kaizen" — it resonates with manufacturing customers.
